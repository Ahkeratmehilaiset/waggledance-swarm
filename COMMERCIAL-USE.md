\# Commercial Use of Protected WaggleDance Modules



Some WaggleDance modules are licensed under \*\*BUSL-1.1\*\*.

Those protected modules are listed in `LICENSE-CORE.md`.



\## You usually do NOT need a commercial license if

\- you are evaluating the project;

\- you are developing or testing internally;

\- you are running CI;

\- you are doing academic or personal research;

\- you are using protected modules only for personal, non-commercial production use.



\## You DO need a commercial license if

\- you ship the protected modules as part of a paid product;

\- you offer the protected modules inside a commercial hosted service or managed service;

\- you use the protected modules for a customer-facing commercial deployment;

\- you build a competitive offering around the protected modules.
